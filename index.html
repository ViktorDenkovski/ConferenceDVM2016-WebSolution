WELCOME

Dear colleagues and friends,

Welcome to the 7th International Scientific Meeting “DAYS OF VETERINARY MEDICINE 2016” to be held in Hotel Izgrev*****, in Struga, R. of Macedonia, from 22th to 24th September, 2016!

Each DAYS OF VETERINARY MEDICINE faces a challenge: to be better than the previous one. This year DVM 2016 is tending to be highly attractive for all researchers, veterinary practitioners, official veterinarians, professors, PhD and post-doctoral students.

The Organizing Committee has already started to work and prepare an interesting and innovative programme, with outstanding social events. The meeting will retain the usual popular format, including a number of invited plenary lectures, short presentations and inspiring workshops. The scientific program will comprise a number of professionally delivered presentations, selected by the Committee based on scientific importance and novelty on the following topics: animal health, welfare and reproduction, as well as public health and food safety.

Struga is a beautiful and easily reachable town in the south-western region of the Republic of Macedonia, lying on the shore of the famous Lake Ohrid.

Hotel Igrev***** is modern and luxurious with 500 meters of private beach and close to both cities of Struga and Ohrid, with a good road connection to the Ohrid airport (15 km).

In short, this will be a wonderful DVM 2016 in terms of both content and ambience!

We look forward to seeing you this year in Struga!

The Organizing Committee,

Venue

Complex Hotel Izgrev, Struga, R. of Macedonia
22-24 September 2016

Set on the Ohrid Lake´s shore and offering an outdoor pool and an indoor pool, Hotel Izgrev Spa & Aquapark is located in Struga. Free WiFi access is available. Free private parking is possible on site.
All rooms are air-conditioned and come with a balcony and a flat-screen TV. Private bathrooms are fitted with a bath tub or with a shower and a hairdryer. You can enjoy lake view from the room. Some units feature a fireplace, a private pool and a safety deposit box.
The on-site à la carte restaurant offers both traditional and international cuisine. Guests can also relax at the bar by the outdoor pool.
At Hotel Izgrev Spa & Aquapark you will find a spa and wellness centre with a hammam, sauna, fitness centre and massage service. Tennis court and mini golf are also available.
